# Escalation Guidelines (B2B AI Support)

## Purpose
To ensure issues are escalated appropriately, with clear context and minimal back-and-forth for engineering and product teams.

---

## When to Escalate Immediately
- Data loss or corruption
- Security or privacy concerns
- AI outputs causing financial or legal risk
- Multi-customer impact
- Prolonged system outages

---

## Information Required for Escalation
- Clear issue summary
- Customer impact and severity
- Reproduction steps (inputs, prompts, configurations)
- Time of occurrence and region
- Logs or screenshots if available

---

## How to Write Effective Escalation Notes
- Be factual and concise
- Avoid emotional language
- Focus on what engineering needs to act
- Separate customer sentiment from technical facts

---

## When Not to Escalate
- Usage limit questions with clear explanations
- Feature requests without blocking impact
- Known issues with active workarounds
