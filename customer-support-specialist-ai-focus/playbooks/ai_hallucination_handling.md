## Principles
- Do not deny the issue
- Do not blame the user
- Do not over-promise model accuracy

## Recommended Language
"The model may generate plausible-sounding information when inputs are ambiguous."

## Support Actions
- Collect prompt + output
- Identify missing constraints
- Suggest prompt refinement
- Escalate repeated failures

## When to Escalate
- Financial or legal implications
- Repeated incorrect outputs
- Enterprise customers affected
