## Case ID
_# UNEXPECTED USAGE CHARGE (Case 006)_

## Customer Message
"We were charged significantly more this month. The usage numbers don’t align with our expectations."

## Category
Billing / Usage

## Context
B2B AI product — usage-based pricing

## Severity
SEV-2 (High)

## Initial Assessment
- Potential mismatch between actual usage and customer expectations.
- May be caused by increased activity, automation, or misunderstanding of billing metrics.
- Initial assessment based on billing report; requires detailed usage review.

## Clarifying Questions
- Which billing period is affected?
- Have new workflows or automations been enabled?
- Are API calls or background processes running?
- Can we review a usage breakdown together?

## Draft Customer Response
Thanks for raising this — I understand unexpected charges can be concerning.

Our billing reflects actual usage across your account. I’d like to review the usage breakdown with you to identify where the increase occurred and confirm whether it aligns with your configuration.

## Internal Notes
- Review usage and billing logs
- Check for recent feature enablement
- Confirm pricing calculations

## Workaround (If Applicable)
- Disable non-essential automation temporarily
- Set internal usage alerts if supported

## Escalation Decision
Yes - escalate to billing support if discrepancy persists after usage review.
