# Severity Levels

## SEV-1 (Critical)
- Production outage
- Data loss or corruption
- Security or privacy risk
- Multiple customers blocked

Immediate escalation required.

## SEV-2 (High)
- Core functionality degraded
- AI output unusable for customer workflow
- Time-sensitive business impact

Escalate within same business day.

## SEV-3 (Medium)
- Partial feature failure
- Workaround available
- Limited customer impact

Monitor and respond within SLA.

## SEV-4 (Low)
- UX issues
- Feature questions
- Enhancement requests

No escalation required.
